Author's Role

The author of a submission is able to log into the journal's web site to track the progress of the submission and participate in the editorial process. If there is more than one author, the author submitting the item is asked to identify the principal contact who becomes the one with whom all editorial correspondence is conducted and who accesses the journal web site.
